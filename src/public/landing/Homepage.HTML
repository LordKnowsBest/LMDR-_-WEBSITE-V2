Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LMDR's AI-powered matching platform.

For Drivers

Is LMDR really free for drivers?

Yes, 100% free. We're paid by carriers looking to fill seatsdrivers never pay a dime. No hidden fees, no catches. Our business model is built on successful placements, so your success is our success.

How fast will I hear from carriers?

Most drivers receive their first contact within 24 hours of completing their profile. Our AI matches you with carriers actively hiring for your experience level and preferred routesso you're not waiting around.

What CDL types do you work with?

We match drivers across Class A, Class B, and Class C CDL holders. Whether you're OTR, regional, local, or specialized (tanker, hazmat, doubles/triples), our platform finds opportunities tailored to your credentials.

Will my info be sold to recruiters?

Never. Your data stays protected. Only carriers that match your preferences can see your profileno mass blasts, no spam calls. You control who reaches out, not the other way around.

Can I specify home time requirements?

Absolutely. During profile setup, you set your home time needsweekly, bi-weekly, or flexible. Our AI only matches you with carriers whose routes align with your schedule preferences. Family first.

How does AI actually help me find a job?

Our AI analyzes your complete profileCDL type, endorsements, experience level, preferred lanes, home time needs, and pay expectationsthen cross-references this against 150+ carrier requirements in real-time. Instead of you scrolling through hundreds of generic job posts, the algorithm surfaces only the opportunities where you're genuinely qualified AND the carrier's offering matches what you're looking for. It's precision matching, not keyword spam.

For Carriers

What does the 48-hour guarantee mean?

We commit to presenting qualified, pre-screened driver candidates within 48 hours of receiving your requirements. If we don't deliver, you don't pay. That's the LMDR promisespeed without sacrificing quality.

How does your vetting process work?

Every driver profile is verified: CDL status, MVR checks, employment history, and endorsement validation. Our AI cross-references your lane requirements with driver preferences and qualificationsso you only see candidates who fit.

What if a driver leaves within 30 days?

Free replacement. No questions asked. If a placed driver doesn't work out within the first 30 days, we restart the search at no additional cost. We stand behind every match we make.

How is pricing structured?

We offer both per-placement and subscription models depending on your hiring volume. Contact our team for a customized quotepricing is transparent, competitive, and designed to deliver ROI from day one.

Can you handle high-volume hiring?

Yes. Our AI-powered platform scales effortlessly. Whether you need 5 drivers or 50, we maintain the same speed and quality standards. Enterprise solutions available for fleets with ongoing recruitment needs.

How does your AI matching work for carriers?

You tell us exactly what you needCDL class, endorsements, experience minimums, lane requirements, home time policies, and pay range. Our AI engine continuously scores incoming driver profiles against your criteria, factoring in soft matches like geographic proximity to your terminals and historical retention patterns. The result: you receive a ranked shortlist of candidates who actually fit your operation, not a flood of unqualified resumes. We're cutting your screening time by 60% while improving match quality.
