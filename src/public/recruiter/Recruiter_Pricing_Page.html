Frequently Asked Questions

Do you integrate with legacy ATS platforms?

Yes. While LMDR is designed to replace your fragmented stack with a unified OS, our Enterprise plan includes API connectors to push data to legacy systems if required by your compliance team.

How does the AI Ad Studio work?

Our "Nano Banana" engine uses generative AI to create high-converting visuals tailored to driver demographics. You input the truck type and location; we generate the compliant, optimized creative for Facebook and Instagram.

Is the Telemetry (VoIP) feature compliant?

Absolutely. All calls are recorded with consent (configurable), and transcripts are stored securely within the candidate's CRM record for quality assurance and training.
