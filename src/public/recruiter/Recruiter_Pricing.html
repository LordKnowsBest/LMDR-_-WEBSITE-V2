Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between job posts and driver search?

Job posts let drivers find and apply to you. Driver search lets YOU find drivers proactively - browse profiles, see match scores, and reach out directly to candidates who fit your requirements.

What counts as a "profile view"?

A profile view is when you open a driver's full profile to see their details, experience, and contact information. Viewing the same driver multiple times in a day only counts as one view.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade instantly and we'll prorate your billing. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. Cancel anytime with no penalties.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No contracts. All plans are month-to-month. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you in.
