Common Questions

What happens when I level up?
You'll see a celebration notification and your new level badge will appear on your profile. Higher levels show recruiters that you're an active, engaged driver - which can help you stand out!
What if I miss a day and lose my streak?
You get 1 streak freeze per month that automatically protects your streak if you miss a day. If you've already used it, don't worry - you can start building a new streak right away!
Can recruiters see my achievements?
Yes! Your level, featured badges, and key achievements are visible on your profile. Recruiters often prioritize engaged drivers with complete profiles and active participation.
How do seasonal events work?
Throughout the year, we run special events like "January Hiring Kickoff" or "Summer Road Trip" where all XP is multiplied (1.5x-2x). Events also have exclusive challenges and badges you can only earn during that time!
Where can I see my referral link?
Go to your Driver Dashboard and look for the "Refer a Friend" section. You can copy your unique link to share with other drivers.
