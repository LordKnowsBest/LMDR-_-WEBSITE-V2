# Game of Vacation: Gamification Strategy Outline

> High-level gamification framework for LMDR platform - Drivers & Recruiters

---

## Executive Summary

Transform the LMDR job matching experience into an engaging journey where both Drivers and Recruiters earn rewards, unlock achievements, and progress through meaningful milestones. The goal: increase platform engagement, improve match quality, and accelerate time-to-hire.
