Directional Shadow Logic

Shadows are cast at +6px, +6px with a soft blur. Highlights are generated at -6px, -6px using pure white. This simulates a top-left illumination source that creates the characteristic "extruded" or "recessed" look.

LIGHT SOURCE Top-Left (-X, -Y)
SHADOW CAST Bottom-Right (+X, +Y)
